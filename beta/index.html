By Liam Mailley

URL

semgeeks.com

Triggers

  • Hover
  • Click/Tap

There are no rules that are not also feedback in this microinteraction.

  • On hover, Cursor changes to pointer
  • Button initially only contains the word 'Explore'
  • Hovering over button causes the words "Digital Marketing Solutions", as well as an arrow, to fade in
  • Text and arrow stay centered
  • Button appears to indent on click/tap
  • After 0.7s, if a user stays hovered on the button, a drop down menu will appear with 7 text links
  • Upon unhovering, "Digital Marketing Solutions" and the arrow will fade out
  • The dropdown menu will also retract upon unhovering
  • On mobile devices only, tapping on the main button will retract the menu and make "Digital Marketing Solutions" and the arrow fade away
  • On mobile devices only, when tapped, "Digital Marketing Solutions" and the arrow will fade in and the menu will appear, since hover is not available
